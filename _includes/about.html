Interested in the Women In Computer Science Club?

We are Women in Computer Science, a student organization at Winona State University open to all students.

We work to promote and support the growing community of women in CS and technology by encouraging discussion and raising awareness of the issues they face. WICS serves as a group to bring together women who share common interests, concerns, and ambitions, within and outside of the computer science field.
